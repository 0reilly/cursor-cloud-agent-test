# Patterns Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 5, 2025

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Patterns (“we,” “our,” or “us”). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application Patterns (the “App”). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

The App is designed to educate users about dark patterns in digital products. We aim to minimize data collection and respect your privacy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Optional Feedback:** If you contact us via email or in-app feedback, we may collect your email address and any content you provide.

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data:** We may collect anonymous analytics about how you interact with the App, such as which screens you view, features you use, and session duration. This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you.
- **Device Information:** We may collect non‑personal device information such as operating system version, device model, and language settings to improve compatibility and performance.

### 2.3 Advertising
The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain information to serve relevant ads and measure ad performance. This may include:
- Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
- IP address
- Non‑personal information about your device and app usage

For details about how Google uses data collected through AdMob, please review Google’s Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

## 3. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:
- To provide, maintain, and improve the App’s functionality and user experience.
- To display advertisements and measure their effectiveness.
- To analyze aggregated usage trends and fix technical issues.
- To respond to your inquiries and provide support.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share non‑personal, aggregated, or anonymized data for analytical purposes.

We may share information with service providers who assist us in operating the App, such as:

These partners are contractually obligated to protect your information and may only use it as necessary to provide their services.
